Document Selection

The dropdown list includes the titles of all of the poems, essays, and introductions in the Swinburne corpus.

An "All documents" options is available at the top of the dropdown list.

Use the "Add Docs" button to move selected titles or the "All" option to the Query set.

The Document dropdown list appears for Document-Document searches. All the chunks in selected documents are processed during the search.

To narrow the search to particular chunks rather than whole documents, use Query with Chunks and its selection interface, Chunk Selection.
